# Professional Developer Certification

More information

More information can also be found on our [certification page](https://www.hyva.io/certification) on hyva.io.

The **Hyvä Professional Developer Certification** requires existing Magento experience in relevant frontend areas. Hyvä project experience is required. It is designed so it can be passed after self-study of documentation (with access to Hyvä source code) or after completion of a training course in addition to **one or more years of hands-on project experience**.

The Professional Certification is a chance to prove your skills, boost your credibility, and show you're experience in building fast, clean, high-quality Magento stores.

For developers, it is a proof of their Hyvä experience and knowledge.
For companies hiring talent, certification is a good way to distinguish potential candidates.

Check the [Professional Developer Certification Guide](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1NiWQOYQhxI6WZk6KzlY0toy7fA1FMHNq/view?usp=sharing) for more information about the topics covered by the exam.

After passing the exam, you will receive a PDF certificate and a [Credly badge](https://www.credly.com/org/hyva-themes-b-v/badge/hyva-certified-professional-developer) after passing the exam.
