# The Hyvä Certification Program

Available Certifications:

- [Hyvä Associate Developer Certification](associate-developer-certification.html)
- [Hyvä Professional Developer Certification](professional-developer-certification.html)

## Certification FAQ:

Does the Certification have an expiration date?

No, but it specifies the year when you passed and provides information on how long ago the required knowledge was demonstrated.

Is the certification tied to the agency or the individual?

The certification is tied to an individual.

Can the exam be taken online?

Yes, you can take the test whenever and wherever you want through our online testing provider Kryterion. There also will be the opportunity to take a test session at select Magento and Hyvä events.

What are the system requirements?

Please refer to the [Kryterion Online Testing Requirements](https://support.kryterion.com/en_us/online-testing-requirements:-what-you-need-to-know-ry42HLJsJl) for more information.
Most importantly for some users: Linux is not supported. Windows, Mac, or a supported Chromebook is required.

Is the exam proctored?

Yes, the online exams are AI and human-proctored.

Where can I get training?

At the time of writing, the [Hyvä developer documentation](https://docs.hyva.io/) is the best starting point. Each certification also has a study guide listing the topics covered by the exam. Additional educational videos will be released in the future.

How to prepare for taking the test?

Be sure to check the [Online Proctored Guidance information](https://support.kryterion.com/en_us/categories/online-proctored-guidance-BJ7MI_M9ye) for Webassessor before starting the test.
Be sure to use a **clear desk** in case the proctor asks you to show your surroundings.

What is the price?

Current certification pricing information can be found on our [certification page](https://www.hyva.io/certification).

Can certifications be purchased in bulk?

Yes, multiple exam vouchers can be purchased at once on our [certification page](https://www.hyva.io/certification).

Can I cancel or reschedule an exam?

Exams can be rescheduled up to 24 hours before the selected time without additional cost. Cancelling or rescheduling later requires booking a new slot at full price. This also applies if a scheduled exam is missed for any reason.

What is Webassessor?

Webassessor is an online platform developed by Kryterion, our testing system provider, a leading player in the market for providing and managing exams.

How do I schedule an exam?

1. Purchase exam vouchers on [our website](https://www.hyva.io/hyva-certification.html).
2. Visit [Webassessor](https://webassessor.com/HYVA) and create an account.
3. Register for the exam and select a date and time.
4. Apply the voucher during checkout on the payment step.

How long are certification vouchers valid?

Vouchers are valid for one year.

Is there a discount for retakes?

Yes, retakes can be purchased at a discounted rate.
The email you receive after the certification attempt contains further information.

How long until I can try again?

The Associate Developer Certification can be retried **one day** after the first attempt.
A second retry can be scheduled **two days** after the second attempt.
A third retry can be scheduled **two days** after the third attempt.
A final retry can be scheduled **a week** after the fourth attempt.

*Note: the retry policy is subject to change.*

Do I get a certificate after passing?

You will receive a PDF certificate and a [Credly Badge](https://www.credly.com/organizations/hyva-themes-b-v/badges) after passing the exam.

Where can I list my Hyvä Certified badge in the Hyvä Portal?

You can list your badge by **linking your Credly URL** in your Hyvä Account. To do this, go to the **Account Information** section and add your Credly profile link.
Once linked, your certification will be visible in the Portal.
