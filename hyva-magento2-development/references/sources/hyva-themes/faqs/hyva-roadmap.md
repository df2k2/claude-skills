# Is there a roadmap? When will X be released?

This applies to all Hyvä products.

Please, refer to the [public roadmap](https://www.hyva.io/roadmap) on our website.

Don't expect release dates, though.

The reason we work that way is because we prefer to have the time it takes to get things right, rather than releasing products prematurely.
It also allows us to change priorities depending on market shifts, without breaking promises.

That said, if you are an official Hyvä Partner, you are able to influence our internal roadmap by opening issues in the respective GitLab repositories and gathering upvotes (thumbs-up reactions) for them.
If an issue gains many thumbs up, we will certainly consider moving it up in our internal priorities strongly.
