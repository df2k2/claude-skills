# Hyvä Portal Overview

The **Hyvä Portal** is your personal and team-based account area at [hyva.io](https://hyva.io). Register for free to manage your commercial and open source licenses, view order history, track certifications, and get technical support. It's your hub for everything Hyvä—whether you're a solo developer or part of an agency partner team.

## Understanding User Roles and License Ownership

The Hyvä Portal works with two core concepts: who owns a license and what team role you have. These are independent—you might own one license while being a team member on another account. Let's break down how each works.

### License Ownership

Any registered user can own a license, regardless of team membership. License ownership is what matters for making changes to a license. Only the owner can:

- Transfer the license to another person
- Change the license domain
- Renew the license
- Cancel automated renewal
- Upgrade to Hyvä Commerce

You don't need to belong to a team to own or manage a license. If you're in a team, you can still personally own licenses—every license is attached to a single customer account.

### Agency Team Roles

If your company is a Hyvä partner, you'll be added to a team. Team roles determine what you can see and do within team settings.

**Members** have access to:

- Their own licenses and personal information
- Licenses owned by other team members (visibility)
- Verified email status of team members

**Managers** have all member access, plus:

- Ability to invite and remove team members
- Power to assign roles (upgrade members to managers, or demote managers to members)
- Control over the company description and profile
- Authority to manage technical support access

Only managers can update company information, manage team membership, and initiate support requests for paid licenses.

### Combining Roles: Owner and Team Member

You can be both a license owner and a team member (or manager) at the same time. Your license management abilities depend on ownership, while your team management abilities depend on your team role. For example, a team manager might own personal licenses, see team licenses, and manage company settings all at once.

### Email Verification for Team Access

To use team features, your email address must be verified if you haven't been added by your team manager(s). You'll find verification links on the **Dashboard** and the **Hyvä Licenses** pages.

Here's how verification works:

1. Click the verification link (available on the Dashboard or Hyvä Licenses page)
2. Hyvä sends you an automated email with a confirmation link
3. Click the link in the email to validate your address
4. After validation, you unlock all restricted team features

## Portal Sections

Each section of the Hyvä Portal serves a specific purpose:

- **[Dashboard](dashboard.html)** - Overview of your account, licenses, and certifications
- **[Hyvä Licenses](licenses.html)** - Manage licenses, transfer, renew, and upgrade
- **[My Orders](orders.html)** - View your order history
- **[Partner Program Overview](partner-program-overview.html)** - Track your partner tier, points, and progress (managers only)
- **[Contributions](contributions.html)** - Submit and manage content contributions that earn partner points (managers only)
- **[Partner Page Entry](partner-page-entry.html)** - Update your agency profile on the partner listing (managers only)
- **[My Agency](my-agency.html)** - Configure your account type and agency relationship
- **[My Clients](my-clients.html)** - Manage client relationships and invitations (managers only)
- **[Team Members](team-members.html)** - Manage team membership and roles (managers only)
- **[Company Description](company-description.html)** - Update your agency profile (managers only)
- **[Account Information](account-information.html)** - Personal settings and certifications
- **[Address Book](address-book.html)** - Manage company addresses and billing
- **[Technical Support](technical-support.html)** - Get help and access support options
