# Introduction Video

Goran Horvat from Bemeir joins us today to introduce the Hyvä Widgets and explain why we built them and how to use them.

We're going through a bit of the history behind widgets and PageBuilder and why some agencies prefer using widgets for content.

Then we go through the features of the Hyvä Widgets and show how to implement and customize them.
